whoami

I am a young computer scientist, that discovered the unix world circa 2019 ever since there I have been a pain to my friends and relatives every time a discussion regarding operating systems starts (I have to admit that is not very often).

I have had very good unix mentors that have teach me the unix way, I am constantly learning new things and try to do better. I'm currently in the stage where I'm tinkering with the idea of moving away from linux (and systemd for that matter) into freebsd for my workstation and openbsd for my web servers.

I am obsessed with code-style, I really hate messy looking code. I try to stick to the linux kernel code-style guidelines and if you code with me I will try to make you use them as well. As you can imagine it can be sometimes annoying to work with me, but I am pretty sure you will thank me later when we have to maintain or add features to the code down the line.

I also try to do code in a pragmatic way sticking to principles like KISS (keep it simple and stupid, or keep it simple, stupid), and DRY (do not repeat yourself)

I also like the idea of the small web and really do not like javascript bloated websites (you know which ones, the ones that take eons to just load some text and pictures). That's why this page is purely html and a small css file.

Also I am not a native english speaker so forgive any grammatical mistakes you find.

Lastly, thank you for reading the blog and wondering who am I. I hope you found it helpful or useful :)
